andrew david music

homegrown organic jams

Heavily inspired by and enamored with the Grateful Dead, I strive to create the type of music that I love to see live; happy and danceable.

I consider myself a bit of a chameleon. Confident as a bass player, drummer, rhythym guitarst, and vocalist, I can easily adapt my music to any situation, whether that entails playing a Grateful Dead cover at a dive bar in Somerville, or an Usher cover busking in Central Square in Cambridge.

More than anything, I love to write. I finished my first song in 2013 (soundcloud player above) and from there have written close to 50 songs.

As an avid hiker and aspiring wunderlust, I am heavily inspired by the outdoors and traveling.
